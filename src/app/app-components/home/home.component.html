Fiction writing is the hardest discipline

And good fiction is even harder. Writer's Sauce is a tool that takes an opinionated approach to writing stories that lend themselves to scene-based narration. It is developed with fiction in mind, but any writing can benefit. It is an applied mathematical approach to spark creativity.

Opinionated

The tool imposes a scene based writing approach using the PELT model. Scenes are made up of people triggering or responding to events that occur at some location where the characters may use or have access to things such as food, cars, weapons, evidence, computers, etc… to act or be acted upon.

Consistent

Keeping track of time; understanding the effects of weather on visibility; and what resources are readily available in the current setting are features that bind the characters. How the main character's personal plot line intertwines with the main plot line determines where story milestones intersect.

Rigourous

Human beings live in loops called habitual domains. States that allow them to discharge stress by accessing resources they are familiar with and can access. The character struggles, tries to adept, fails and eventually finds a new habitual domain that has those resources. At the end of the story, the main character is a new person--real in the eyes of the reader.
